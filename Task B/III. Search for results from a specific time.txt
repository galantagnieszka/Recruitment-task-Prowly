III. Title: Search for results from a specific time 

Environment: https://www.google.com/

Steps:
1. Type into the input search: prowly. 
2. Click the enter or search icon. 
3. Click on the button “tools”. 
4. Select in a time dropdown option “Past month”.
5. Check if the search results are from last month.

Expected result:
The search results are from the last month.
