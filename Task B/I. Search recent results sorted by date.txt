I. Title: Search recent results sorted by date

Environment: https://www.google.com/

Steps:
1. Type into the search input: prowly.
2. Click the enter or search icon.
3. Choose the filter “News”. 
4. Click on the button “tools”.
5. In a filter “sorted by” choose “date”.
6. In a filter with time select “Recent”. 
7. Check if selected filters are applied properly. 

Expected result:
Searching should show results sorted by date from the newest.
