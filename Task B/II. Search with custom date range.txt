II. Title: Search with custom date range

Environment: https://www.google.com/

Steps:
1. Type into the search input: prowly.
2. Click the enter or search icon. 
3. Click on the button “tools”.
4. In a dropdown related to the time select the option “custom range”.
5. In a calendar/inputs select/type a range from: 7/1/2023 to: 7/31/2023.
6. Check if the searched results are from this range. 

Expected result:
The search results are from the appropriate date range.
